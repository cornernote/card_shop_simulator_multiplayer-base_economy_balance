[h1]Base Economy Balance[/h1]

[b]Base Economy Balance[/b] retunes card values, pack rarity odds, rarity multipliers, and premium traits so opening packs feels better without replacing card art, card names, rarity, stats, elements, or pack placement.

[h2]=== FEATURES ===[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Fun pack-opening balance[/b] - standard packs should feel worth opening without becoming a low-cost cash printer.
[*][b]Smoother card values[/b] - commons, uncommons, rares, and super rares scale more cleanly across gens 1-7.
[*][b]Smoother pack odds[/b] - premium packs are less pure miss-or-jackpot chaos, with rare luxury kept as a chase tier.
[*][b]Smoother rarity multipliers[/b] - common-heavy standard packs are not constant misses.
[*][b]Gentler premium traits[/b] - luxury and rare luxury packs are less dominated by giant jackpot frames.
[*][b]Chase cards preserved[/b] - rare, super rare, god, holiday, and souvenir cards still matter.
[*][b]Visual-mod friendly[/b] - works with card art/name replacement mods because it preserves card identity and art.
[*][b]Safe registry usage[/b] - uses the known safe card registry methods and avoids unsafe object reflection.
[/list]

[h2]=== BALANCE TARGET ===[/h2]

The goal is not strict anti-profit balance. The goal is that opening packs is fun without turning packs into reliable money printers:

[list]
[*]Standard packs should feel okay to open without becoming a reliable sealed-value exploit.
[*]Luxury packs sit close to sealed-pack market value, sometimes above.
[*]Rare luxury packs are the clear open-over-sell chase tier.
[*]Holiday/event packs remain premium because they are drops, not ordinary shop purchases.
[*]The tuning leaves room for free map cards, achievement rewards, limited sale slots, and cat sales below market value.
[/list]

[h2]=== COMPATIBILITY ===[/h2]

This mod preserves card identity and changes [b]CardValueMulti[/b] plus pack rarity odds and global rarity/trait tables. It should work with vanilla cards and visual replacement mods such as GenMTG.

[h2]=== NOTES ===[/h2]

The game does not currently expose getters for vanilla pack drop tables, final card instance pricing, frame weights, or premium variant odds through the safe Lua registry surface. This mod sets balanced pack rarity rates, trait rates, rarity values, trait values, and editable base card values.
